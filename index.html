Development of SSIE Master Programme

The Secure and Safe Intelligent Embedded Systems (SSIE) Master’s programme at EPITA is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge to develop robust and secure embedded systems. This innovative curriculum not only emphasises the principles of safety and security in embedded systems design, but also explores the exciting intersection of artificial intelligence and embedded technologies.
